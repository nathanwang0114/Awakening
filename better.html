THE BETTER FUTURE

Earth has been providing for us for the longest time, and we have been neglecting her. If the government, companies, and all of us act now and work together to preserve our beloved Earth, we will have "The Better Future". This is what it holds for us.

- We will still feel the effects of climate change, but it will be much less severe and more manageable.

- We will be able to adapt to our new enviornment and transition over to a greener society.

- Sea level rise will be less of an issue, as we will have more time to move people from coastal communities away from high risk areas.

- The rich will help the poor fight off the negatives impacts on them caused by climate change.

- Emphasis on protecting forests and growing more trees to help suck the carbon out of the air. Indigenous groups can be leaders of this due to their experiences.

- Food shortage and extreme weather conditions like droughts and wildfires will be minimalized.

- Everybody will have a greener diet, switching over to a less meat heavy diets.

- Farms will focus on growing grops suitable to the current weather conditions, and use sustainable farming techniques.

- Switch over to wind and solar power, and creating new technologies/using materials that emit less carbon emissions.

- Air quality will be improved since everyone will switch over to electric vehicles or use green transportation which will be much more efficient and convenient than present day.

- Less likely to get sick with better air quality, living conditons, and healthier lifestyles.

All of these solutions are implementable today and the costs to implement is getting cheaper and cheaper. There is only one Earth, it is now or never for us to make a change. If we don't act now, there will be no stopping "The Bad Future" from coming.
