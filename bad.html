THE BAD FUTURE

If we keep going on the path we are going on, ignoring climate change and destroying Earth, then this is how the future is going to look.

- Life will be harder for the majority of us, except the rich who will still get to live comfortablty. People will look for a better enviornment to live, causing housing to skyrocket in price.

- Life for living organisms will also be much harder, leading to extinctions and less life on Earth.

- Earth will be hotter in most places, leading to many more heatwaves that cancel school and activites hosted outdoors. The older generation is especially supsceptible to heatwaves.

- We will be at a food shortage due to the weather conditions making it harder to grow crops. We will also try to cut down on meat, since animals like cows produce lots of CO2.

- More air pollution caused by green house gasses and wildfires occuring much more often.

- Fresh water becomes more scarce caused by droughts and sea level rising seeping into our fresh water sources, causing erosions and destroying marshland habitats.

- Sea level rise will also cause flooding of coastal communities, causing many communities and homes to be wrecked and needing assistance and a new place to stay.

- Ocean water becomes more acidic and warm after absorbing all the carbon from the air. This will destroy many habitats like coral reefs, and cause some of the marine animals we depend on as a food source to be harmed.

All these things will be bound to happen in our future no matter what we do now due to the carbon that is already in the air, but we can still prevent this worse future from happening if we act now.
