SOLUTIONS TO CLIMATE CHANGE

Here are some various solutions you can incorporate in your day to day life to help end climate change. By incorporating these simple solutions into your lifestyle, you can drop your carbon footprint by a significant ammount!

Save Energy At Home

Much of our electricity and heat are powered by coal, oil, and gas. To reduce this usage, you can:

- use the heater and air condition less.

- switch to LED lights and energy effecient appliances.

- turn off the lights, faucets, or appliances that use electricity when not in use.

- use cold wash and air dry for your laundry.

Green Transportation

When we commute we think of driving or plane rides. However, with so many vehicles on our roads and planes burning fossil fuels, there are greener alternatives such as:

- walking, biking, scootering

- carpooling, public transport

- decreasing the amount of flights you take

Greener Diet

Livestock production produces a big portion of CO2 emissions, we should cut down on meat and dairy products and instead:

- switch to plant based agriculture, saving land, energy, water, and producing less CO2.

- eat more fruits, whole grains, vegetables, seeds, and nuts.

- cut food waste and compost your leftovers if you have too.

Reuse, Repair, & Recycle

New goods we buy all come from factories, which causes a lot of carbon emissions when making them. Try:

- shopping for second hand.

- repairing what you can and recycle.

- buying less goods.

Green Products to Invest In

Help reduce carbon emissions by switching over to green energy when looking to renovate your home or looking for a new vehicle.

- Researching about a company before buying their goods.

- Investing in solar power or wind power.

- Switching over to an electric vehicle.

Last, but not least, speak up and help promote climate change in your local community!
